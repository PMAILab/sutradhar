Sutradhar Privacy Policy

Last updated July 2026

Sutradhar is a tool for wedding planners. This page explains what information we collect, why, and how it is used. It applies to the Sutradhar web app and to messages sent through Sutradhar's WhatsApp integration.

What we collect

When a planner signs up, we collect their name, email address, and login credentials. When a planner creates a wedding in Sutradhar, we store the details they enter: ceremony information, vendor names and phone numbers, family contact details, and the messages exchanged with vendors and family over WhatsApp.

How we use it

We use this information to run the product: to structure a client brief into a ceremony plan, to check that plan for gaps, and to send and track WhatsApp messages to vendors and family on the planner's behalf. We do not sell this information to anyone.

WhatsApp messaging

Sutradhar sends WhatsApp messages through Meta's WhatsApp Cloud API, using templates that vendors and family contacts have agreed to receive as part of working with the planner. Replies are read by Sutradhar to track confirmation status and are visible only to the planner who owns that wedding.

Who can see planner data

Each planner can only see their own weddings, vendors, and messages. Data is not shared across planner accounts.

Data retention

We keep wedding data for as long as the planner's account is active. A planner can request deletion of their account and its data at any time by contacting us.

Contact

Questions about this policy or a request to delete your data can be sent to privacy@sutradhar.app.
